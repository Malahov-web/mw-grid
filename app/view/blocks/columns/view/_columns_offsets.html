Columns offsets

Columns can has offsets (from left), and we have classes like ".ds_offset_6" to moving columns

  • Offsets sizes are the same as columns sizes
  • Offsets is CSS-property margin-left
  • Offsets are responsive (".tb_offset_6.ds_offset_4") up to devices
  • Offsets can be reset (".tb_offset_2.ds_offset_0")

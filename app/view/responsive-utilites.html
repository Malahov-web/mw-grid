Responsive utilites

Mw-grid has 2 utilites for responsive display (show/hide) elements. They are CSS-classes ".*hidden" and ".*visible", which add display: none;

'Hidden' show on small and hide on large up from breakpoint (Hide up)
'Visible' show on large and hide on small down from breakpoint (Hide down)

  • ".*hidden" use media min-width to hide
  • ".*visible" use media max-width to hide
  • Mix 'Hidden' and 'Visible' to show on one device only
  • These classes add left and right paddings (columns gap) to element. You can use ".collapse" to reset it when you need
